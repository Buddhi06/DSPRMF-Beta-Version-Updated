A Data Security and Privacy Risk Management
Framework for Medical Device Software (MDS) AI Model Lifecycle (Beta Version)

A Risk Management Process for assuring Data Security and Privacy when Developing, Deploying, and Maintaining MDS AI Models

The purpose of this framework is to provide comprehensive guidance on performing a risk management process to address data security and privacy risks that arise during MDS AI model development, deployment, and maintenance, assuring that the data remains secure and private

The four phases of the risk management process (Risk Assessment, Risk Control, Evaluation of Overall Residual Risk Acceptability, and Preparation of the Risk Management Report) provided in this framework correspond those outlined in AAMI TIR57 - Principles for Medical Device Security - Risk Management

The scope of this framework encompasses the "entire MDS AI Model Lifecycle including development, deployment, and maintenance"
